Our Field Service Management(FSM)

QBrainX helps organizations match the tasks of their field agents based on the skills, assignments, inventory, and geographical territory.

Assisting field agents with task management, status, time management, and travel planning for all field-related activities.

Better Customer Visibility
Customers can book appointments and receive text message updates on worker location and arrival time.
Automated Work Assignment
Work assignments are automated based on availability, proximity, and the parts and skills required.
Source and Parts Management
Utilize ServiceNow Asset Management as a centralized repository for assets in stock and inventory control.
Exceptionally Innovative
FSM assigns tasks to agents based on their abilities, geographic territory assignments, and inventory availability.
Seemless Work Orders
Integrate with ServiceNow Connected Operations, Customer Service Management, or IT Service Management to resolve faster.
Enhanced Insights
Use and customize pre-built reports and dashboards to gain real-time insight into utilization, capacity, customers and locations requesting work, and other metrics.
