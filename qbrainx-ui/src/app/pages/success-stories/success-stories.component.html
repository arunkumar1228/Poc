Application as a Service (SaaS) for a Better Consumer Experience

The story of how one of Australia’s most well-known insurance company increased customer engagement while lowering operating costs.

With the increasing importance of insurance behemoths, large insurers are constantly looking for new technologies to improve customer acquisition and engagement. Customers can benefit from such technologies in terms of receiving accurate information and quick assistance. Using QBrainX’s Solution, the customers can use the SaaS-based platform to select/opt for multiple insurances in one place, as well as create their profiles and more.

A leading Australian insurance company created a SaaS-based platform and streamlined a customer self-service portal, giving support staff better workplace technology with the help of QBrainX’s solution.
